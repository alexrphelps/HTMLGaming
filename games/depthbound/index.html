Run Identity

Every run gives the dungeon a personality: Hungry, Clockwork, Fungal, Mirror, Royal, or Abyssal. It changes enemy behavior, room rewards, hazards, and upgrade bias.

Build Depth

Level up into Gunner, Phantom, Occult, Engineer, and Alchemist branches. Stack passives, unlock actives, collect relics, and gamble with curses.

Tip: Click Start Run, then press F on the glowing Dungeon Gate or walk through any door to enter combat. Combat rooms lock until cleared.
