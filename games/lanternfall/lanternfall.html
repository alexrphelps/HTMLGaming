Lanternfall

an infinite cave, charted one step at a time

Somewhere beneath the world lies a cave with no known edges. Carry your lantern, chart the dark, and gather what glimmers in the dust around you.

Move with WASD or arrow keys — or the dial in the corner, if you're charting by touch.

💎 Gems — add to your tally 👢 Boots — quicken your step, briefly 🏮 Lantern oil — widens what you can see 🧭 Compass — points toward the nearest find
Tip: ground you've already charted stays dimly visible — even items you've spotted but not yet claimed.
