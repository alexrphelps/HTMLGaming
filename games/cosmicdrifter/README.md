# Cosmic Drifter 🚀

A 2D open-world exploration and survival game where players control a hover-craft explorer drifting through the remnants of shattered floating worlds in cosmic space.

## 🎮 Game Overview

**Cosmic Drifter** is an immersive space exploration experience that combines survival mechanics with open-world exploration. Players must manage their ship's energy and hull integrity while collecting resources, avoiding hazards, and upgrading their vessel to survive deeper into the unknown cosmos.

## 🎯 Core Features

### Exploration
- **Infinite World**: Procedurally generated cosmic environment with seamless exploration
- **Smooth Camera**: Camera follows the player with smooth interpolation
- **Mini-map**: Real-time scanner showing nearby objects and hazards
- **Distance Tracking**: Track how far you've traveled into the cosmos

### Survival Mechanics
- **Energy System**: Manage your ship's energy - it drains over time and when thrusting
- **Hull Integrity**: Avoid hazards that damage your ship's hull
- **Resource Management**: Collect energy orbs, tech fragments, and crystals
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Deeper exploration increases challenge

### Upgrades & Progression
- **Tech Fragments**: Collect fragments to purchase upgrades
- **Crystals**: Rare resources worth more than fragments
- **Upgrade Stations**: Dock at stations to enhance your ship
- **Multiple Upgrade Paths**: Energy efficiency, shields, scanner range, speed, and capacity

### Visual & Audio
- **Particle Effects**: Thruster trails, collection effects, and damage particles
- **Ambient Stars**: Twinkling starfield background
- **Glowing Objects**: Energy orbs, crystals, and stations with dynamic lighting
- **Sound Effects**: Web Audio API generated sounds for immersion

## 🕹️ Controls

- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**: Move your hover-craft
- **E**: Interact with upgrade stations (when in range)
- **ESC**: Pause the game

## 🎮 Gameplay Loop

1. **Start**: Spawn in a randomly generated cosmic region
2. **Explore**: Move freely through the infinite world
3. **Collect**: Gather energy orbs, tech fragments, and crystals
4. **Survive**: Avoid asteroids and storm hazards
5. **Upgrade**: Visit stations to enhance your ship's capabilities
6. **Progress**: Travel deeper into space for greater challenges

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Launch the game from the GameHub platform
2. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your hover-craft
3. Collect the glowing yellow energy orbs to maintain your energy
4. Gather green tech fragments and blue crystals for upgrades
5. Avoid red hazards (asteroids and storms)
6. Dock at blue upgrade stations to enhance your ship
7. Explore deeper into space for greater challenges!

## 🏆 Tips for Success

- **Energy Management**: Don't thrust unnecessarily - energy is precious
- **Resource Priority**: Crystals are worth 2x fragments, prioritize them
- **Upgrade Strategy**: Energy efficiency and scanner range are great early upgrades
- **Hazard Avoidance**: Use your scanner to plan routes around dangerous areas
- **Station Timing**: Visit upgrade stations when you have enough resources
