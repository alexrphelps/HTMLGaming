# 🪂 Sky Squirrel - Enhancement Roadmap

## 🎯 **Major Enhancement Categories**

### 1. 🎨 **Visual & Audio Enhancements**
**Goal**: Transform the game into a visually stunning and immersive experience

#### **Large Impact Changes:**
- [ ] **3D Wingsuit Model System**
  - Import GLTF wingsuit model with realistic materials
  - Animated wing deployment/retraction
  - Dynamic wing physics based on speed and angle
  - Customizable wingsuit colors and designs

- [ ] **Advanced Particle System**
  - Wind trail particles that follow player movement
  - Dust clouds on takeoff and landing
  - Snow effects in high altitude areas
  - Explosive landing effects with debris
  - Atmospheric particles (clouds, mist, fog)

- [ ] **Comprehensive Sound System**
  - Dynamic wind rushing sounds based on speed
  - 3D positional audio for environmental sounds
  - Footsteps on different terrain types
  - Jump and landing sound effects
  - Ambient mountain audio (birds, wind, echoes)
  - Music system with dynamic tracks

- [ ] **Enhanced Terrain & Environment**
  - High-resolution terrain textures with normal maps
  - Procedural vegetation (trees, grass, rocks)
  - Snow-covered peaks and valleys
  - Water bodies (lakes, rivers) with reflections
  - Dynamic weather system (rain, snow, fog)

- [ ] **Advanced Lighting & Weather**
  - Dynamic day/night cycle
  - Realistic cloud system with shadows
  - Volumetric fog and atmospheric scattering
  - Dynamic weather conditions affecting visibility
  - Sun rays and god rays effects

### 2. 🎮 **Gameplay Mechanics**
**Goal**: Create engaging, challenging, and rewarding gameplay systems

#### **Large Impact Changes:**
- [ ] **Comprehensive Scoring System**
  - Near-miss scoring with terrain proximity detection
  - Trick scoring system (flips, spins, barrel rolls)
  - Distance and altitude achievements
  - Speed-based bonus multipliers
  - Combo system for consecutive tricks
  - Leaderboards with global rankings

- [ ] **Mission & Objective System**
  - Story mode with narrative progression
  - Time-based challenges and races
  - Collection missions (find hidden items)
  - Precision landing challenges
  - Endurance flights (stay airborne as long as possible)
  - Trick completion challenges

- [ ] **Power-up & Enhancement System**
  - Speed boost power-ups
  - Temporary gliding enhancements
  - Altitude recovery items
  - Trick multiplier bonuses
  - Shield for collision protection
  - Time dilation effects

- [ ] **Multiple Level System**
  - Alpine mountains with snow and ice
  - Desert canyons with thermal updrafts
  - Urban environments with buildings
  - Coastal cliffs with ocean views
  - Volcanic landscapes with lava flows
  - Fantasy floating islands

- [ ] **Advanced Trick System**
  - Barrel rolls and corkscrews
  - Backflips and frontflips
  - Spiral dives and recovery
  - Wingtip touches and close calls
  - Formation flying with AI companions
  - Trick combinations and sequences

### 3. ⚡ **Physics & Realism**
**Goal**: Create the most realistic wingsuit flying experience possible

#### **Large Impact Changes:**
- [ ] **Advanced Physics Engine Integration**
  - Integrate Ammo.js or Cannon.js for realistic physics
  - Proper collision detection with complex terrain
  - Realistic body physics and momentum
  - Advanced wing aerodynamics simulation
  - Fluid dynamics for air resistance

- [ ] **Realistic Air Dynamics**
  - Wind direction and strength affecting flight
  - Thermal updrafts and downdrafts
  - Air density changes with altitude
  - Turbulence effects in certain areas
  - Wake turbulence from other objects

- [ ] **Advanced Weather System**
  - Dynamic wind patterns across the map
  - Storm systems with strong winds
  - Temperature effects on air density
  - Precipitation affecting visibility and control
  - Weather forecasting system

- [ ] **Realistic Landing Mechanics**
  - Different landing surfaces (snow, rock, water, grass)
  - Landing impact calculations
  - Roll and slide mechanics
  - Landing gear deployment system
  - Crash physics with damage modeling

- [ ] **Environmental Interactions**
  - Trees and vegetation that can be touched
  - Water splash effects and buoyancy
  - Snow displacement and tracks
  - Rock and debris interactions
  - Dynamic object physics

### 5. 🔧 **Technical Improvements**
**Goal**: Optimize performance, expand compatibility, and add advanced features

#### **Large Impact Changes:**
- [ ] **Performance Optimization**
  - Level-of-detail (LOD) system for terrain and objects
  - Frustum culling and occlusion culling
  - Efficient particle system with GPU acceleration
  - Texture streaming and compression
  - Memory management and garbage collection optimization
  - Multi-threading for physics and AI

- [ ] **Mobile & Cross-Platform Support**
  - Touch controls for mobile devices
  - Gyroscope and accelerometer support
  - Responsive UI for different screen sizes
  - Cross-platform save synchronization
  - Progressive Web App (PWA) features
  - Offline mode with cached content

- [ ] **Advanced Save & Progress System**
  - Cloud save synchronization
  - Multiple save slots and profiles
  - Automatic backup and recovery
  - Progress tracking across sessions
  - Export/import save data
  - Version migration system

- [ ] **Multiplayer & Social Features**
  - Real-time multiplayer races
  - Ghost racing against other players
  - Cooperative missions and challenges
  - Social features (friends, chat, sharing)
  - Tournament and competition system
  - Spectator mode for watching races

- [ ] **Modding & Customization Support**
  - Level editor for custom maps
  - Custom wingsuit and equipment mods
  - Scripting system for custom behaviors
  - Asset import tools
  - Community workshop integration
  - Mod management system
